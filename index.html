01 Must Love Dogs SoCal · San Diego, CA
Sales Process Automation
Must Love Dogs SoCal is a boutique dog wedding attendant company I founded — bringing clients' dogs to their weddings so they can be part of the day without the stress. As a solo operator, admin was consuming 45–60 minutes per new lead. I automated the entire intake funnel.
Before
  • Read each Squarespace inquiry email manually
  • Created ClickUp tasks by hand, every time
  • Reached out to schedule initial sales call
  • Created and filled agenda docs manually
  • Calculated pricing on a spreadsheet (hours + multi-leg mileage)
  • Drafted personalized follow-up emails from scratch
  • Manually checked calendar for Monday availability
  • No dashboard — clients tracked in scattered notes
After
  • Paste lead → AI creates ClickUp task, fills agenda doc, drafts follow-up email with open time slots
  • Type venue + times → full quote with hours and multi-leg mileage in seconds
  • "Add to dashboard" → formatted booking card generated instantly
  • Process docs analyzed → automation suggestions added automatically
  • Entire intake: ~8 minutes
85%
reduction in intake time
4 Claude Skills
built end-to-end

What I built

  • New Lead Intake — Reads the Squarespace form submission, creates a ClickUp task, auto-fills the Initial Call Agenda doc, and drafts a personalized follow-up email with calendar-aware Monday time slots.
  • Pricing Calculator — Input venue, event times, and start location → outputs a full quote with on-site hours and multi-leg mileage (home → getting-ready location → ceremony → home).
  • Client Dashboard — On booking confirmation, generates a formatted dashboard card with couple info, wedding date, dog details, and notes.
  • Process Review — Analyzes any business process doc and appends a page of specific, implementable automation recommendations.

Tools used

Claude (Anthropic) ClickUp Squarespace Google Docs Google Calendar
02 Personal · Active Job Search
Job Search as a System
Instead of manually searching job boards and rewriting resumes from scratch, I built a two-part AI system that scouts for roles daily and produces a full application package — tailored resume PDF, verified LinkedIn contacts, and outreach drafts — in under 30 minutes.
Before
  • Manually searched job boards every day — easy to miss new postings
  • Rewrote resume from scratch per application (2–3 hours)
  • Manually researched LinkedIn contacts at each company
  • Drafted outreach messages one by one
  • No system for tracking what had already been evaluated
  • Interview prep done ad hoc, no structure
After
  • Job Scout runs across 10+ target companies daily — surfaces new roles, profiles, and posts automatically
  • Hell Yes / Maybe / No rating loop → auto-logs everything to ClickUp
  • Tailored resume PDF generated from base profile in minutes
  • LinkedIn contacts researched and verified with outreach drafts ready to send
  • Company brief + interview screener generated on demand

What I built

  • Job Scout Agent — Daily automated search across target companies. Surfaces new job postings, LinkedIn profiles worth connecting with, and posts to comment on. Walks through a Hell Yes / Maybe / No feedback loop and logs everything to ClickUp automatically.
  • Job Search Toolkit — On-demand application engine: evaluates job fit, generates a tailored resume PDF, researches and verifies LinkedIn contacts, drafts personalized outreach messages, and builds a company org brief + interview screener.

03 Cork & Stem · San Diego, CA
Shift Schedule → Google Calendar, Automatically
My employer sends a weekly shift schedule via Square email. I got tired of manually entering each shift into Google Calendar and built a one-command solution.
Before
  • Received shift schedule as a formatted Square email weekly
  • Manually entered each shift into Google Calendar (10–15 min)
  • Occasional typos, missed shifts, wrong times
  • Repeated every single week without fail
After
  • Say "sync my schedule" → AI reads the email, parses shifts, creates all calendar events
  • Correct times, location, and titles — every time
  • Done in under 2 minutes
  • Zero manual calendar entry
